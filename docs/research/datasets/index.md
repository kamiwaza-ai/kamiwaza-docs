# Research Datasets

Access high-quality, well-documented datasets from Kamiwaza AIR to support your agentic AI research and development.

---

## Available Datasets

### Coming Soon

We're preparing our first open-source datasets for release. Our datasets will focus on enabling research in agentic AI systems, with comprehensive documentation and usage examples.

---

## Dataset Categories

Our planned dataset releases cover:

- **Agentic Behavior**: Traces and logs from agentic AI systems
- **Multi-Agent Interactions**: Communication patterns and coordination data
- **Performance Benchmarks**: Standardized test cases for agentic systems
- **Real-World Applications**: Datasets from production AI deployments
- **Evaluation Metrics**: Ground truth data for measuring agentic AI performance

---

## Using Our Datasets

All Kamiwaza AIR datasets will be:

- **Open-source**: Released under permissive licenses
- **Well-documented**: With comprehensive README files and usage guides
- **Reproducible**: Including scripts and tools for data processing
- **Standardized**: Following best practices for dataset publication
- **Community-driven**: With channels for feedback and contributions
